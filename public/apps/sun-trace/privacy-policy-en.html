Sun Trace Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

1. Overview

Sun Trace is a Chrome browser extension that displays sun trajectory on maps. We value your privacy, and this policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

  • What we collect: Latitude and longitude coordinates from your map clicks
  • Purpose: Calculate sun trajectory and sunrise/sunset times
  • Storage: Only stored locally in your browser, never uploaded to servers

2.2 Time Information

  • What we collect: Date and time you select
  • Purpose: Calculate sun position for specific times
  • Storage: Only stored locally in your browser

2.3 Technical Information

  • What we collect: Detection of map service type (Amap, Baidu, Google)
  • Purpose: Ensure extension works properly on map websites
  • Storage: Only stored locally in your browser

3. Information We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • ❌ User account information
  • ❌ Network requests or browsing history
  • ❌ Third-party website content or data
  • ❌ Any sensitive personal information

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Local Processing: All sun trajectory calculations are performed locally in your browser
  • No Server Transmission: We do not send your data to any servers
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your information with third parties
  • Functional Purpose: Data is only used to provide sun trajectory visualization

5. Data Storage

  • Storage Location: All data is stored only locally in your browser
  • Retention Period: Data persists until you clear browser data or uninstall the extension
  • Data Security: We cannot access your locally stored data

6. Permissions Explanation

Permissions requested by this extension and their purposes:

  • content_scripts: Only runs on map websites (Amap, Baidu, Google)
  • web_accessible_resources: Provides map detection scripts
  • No Network Permissions: Extension does not make any network requests

7. Your Rights

  • Data Control: You can clear browser data anytime to delete all information
  • Uninstall Extension: Uninstalling removes all related data
  • No Tracking: We do not track your usage behavior

8. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified through extension updates.
